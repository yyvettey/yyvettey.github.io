## Abstract
We present here the draft genome sequence of a <i>Providencia stuartii</i> strain, derived from the salivary glands of larval <i>Lucilia sericata</i>, a common blow fly important to forensic, medical, and veterinary science. The genome sequence will help dissect coinfections involving <i>P. stuartii</i> and <i>Proteus mirabilis</i>, as well as blow flyâ€“bacteria interactions.
